David Yang rejoins the team

David Yang rejoins the team

Felicity Boveheart, editor of The Daily Burne

March 20, 2016

Breaking News

Greetings competitors,

Last week, Harold Swine - Chairman of the SLPC - opened up the 2016 year by announcing the return of the Swinlympia, now under SLPC promotion. The press conference showered much awe over the Swinburne community as this year’s games will be heralded as the beginning of ‘The New Era’, establishing an open call out to all members of the community to compete in the Swinlympia.

However, last night, current Swinlympia Champion - David Yang - broke his silence on the matter and announced that he too will be returning to Swinburne and will serve as lead promoter for the events, as well appear on commentary. Yang tweeted, ‘Of course i’m back, did you really doubt it? #TheChampIsHere shoutout to @JohnCena’

The Swinlympia has returned!

The Swinlympia has returned!

Felicity Boveheart, editor of The Daily Burne

March 14, 2016

Greetings competitors, and welcome back to another season of the Swinlympia!

After the enormous success of the 2015 season, the Swinlympia is back again this year under a new promoter - The Swinburne Life Preservation Committee, chaired by none other than Swinburne alumni, Harold Swine.

The SLPC hopes to further build upon the Swinlympia’s motto of ‘Health, physique, and mind. Conquer the games, Conquer yourselves!’ by crowning this years champion as the face of the ‘New Era’, as Mr Swine put it during the SLPC press conference earlier this year.

‘I started this committee to breed unity within the university. Swinlympia’s stature and appeal is cornerstone to what I believe Swinburne is about - the preservation of life, not just physical, but mentally as well. It truly is a testament to Swinlympia’s team for designing events that can be played by all walks of life.’ Mr Swine said during his opening remarks at the SLPC press conference.
