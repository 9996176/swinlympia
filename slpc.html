Swinlympia 2016!

Scheduled to start on xxxx, Swinlympia 2016 is a series of games that celebrates the upcoming Rio Olympics 2016 - with the winners receiving respect and admiration for their efforts.

The games will test the mental fortitude, physical prowess, and strategy-building of its contestants, with games scheduled to run once a week.

At the end of the four week event, a winner will be chosen to be forever enshrined in Swinburne legacy as the champion of Swinlympia!

What We Do

Here at the SLPC, it is our mission to let the human race be the best it can be. We want to preserve and grow the best parts of humanity.

Who We Are

Lead by Harold Swine - former student and renown pro-life activist, and backed by other esteemed board members, the Swinburne Life Preservation Committee is run by experts in their respective areas. Although we are a relatively new organisation, we're dedicated to achieving our goals, no matter the size.
